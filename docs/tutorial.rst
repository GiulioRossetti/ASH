Contents (synthetic extract)
----------------------------

Basics
^^^^^^
Creation of an ASH object, addition of nodes and hyperedges, access to stars and basic measures.

Attribute analysis
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
Purity/entropy measures on hyperedge profiles, homogeneity, and temporal consistency of attributes.

s-Walks
^^^^^^^
Computation of s-walks, distances, and s-based components in the temporal context.

Generators
^^^^^^^^^^
Examples of random hypernetwork generators and transformations from external structures.

I/O
^^^
Reading and writing ASH structures in various formats (CSV profiles, full JSON, etc.).
